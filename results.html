Findings and Outcomes

Technical Feasibility

The pilot systems for glass recycling and biogas production demonstrated consistent performance, confirming the technical feasibility of the integrated waste management approach.

Economic Impact

A cost-benefit analysis showed that the project is financially viable. Revenue from biogas and recycled glass sand can offset waste management costs, making it a sustainable solution.

Environmental Benefits

  • Reduction in landfill waste
  • Lower greenhouse gas emissions from food waste decomposition
  • Conservation of natural sand resources through glass recycling

User Feedback

Stakeholders found the waste management software intuitive and effective in tracking waste generation and estimating resource value, improving overall waste management efficiency.
